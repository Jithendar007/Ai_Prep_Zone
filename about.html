About AI Prepzone

AI Prepzone was created to be a smart, simple, and effective exam preparation partner for students. By leveraging the power of generative AI, we provide instant, customized quizzes on any topic, helping you test your knowledge and prepare for success.

Key Features

  • Instant Quiz Generation on Any Topic
  • Customizable Difficulty and Question Count
  • Interactive UI with Instant Feedback
  • Score Tracking & Answer Review
  • Integrated AI Chatbot for Assistance
